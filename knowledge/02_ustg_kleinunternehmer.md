# UStG §19 — Kleinunternehmerregelung (Small Business Regulation)

**Source:** Umsatzsteuergesetz (UStG), Section 19
**URL:** https://www.gesetze-im-internet.de/ustg_1980/__19.html

## Text (excerpt, translated)

Value added tax otherwise owed on transactions under Section 1(1) no. 1 is not levied on
resident entrepreneurs where turnover under Section 19(1), plus the tax applicable to it,
did not exceed EUR 25,000 in the preceding calendar year and is not expected to exceed
EUR 100,000 in the current calendar year (thresholds as amended effective 1 January 2025;
prior years used EUR 22,000 and EUR 50,000 respectively).

An entrepreneur applying this regulation may not deduct input VAT (Vorsteuerabzug) under
Section 15, and may not separately state VAT on invoices. An entrepreneur may waive
application of this regulation by declaration to the tax office (Verzichtserklärung); the
waiver binds the entrepreneur for five calendar years.

## Practical note

For a business started mid-year, the EUR 25,000 threshold is not pro-rated in the same way
turnover forecasts are; the actual first-year turnover is compared directly against the
threshold, and the following year's forecast against the EUR 100,000 ceiling.
